About Us

Remeber the last time you sat down with a bowl of popcorn to watch a bad movie and thought, "I could do this way better"? Well we did that! Popcorn Productions (P.P.) is all about producing the best content for the best viewing experience. We hope our movies are good on their own, but we recommend you enjoy them with some delicious, warm, buttery popcorn at your side. Maybe a refreshing beverage, too.

Our passion for movie making stems from being just like you, wanting to see our dreams on screen. Thanks to you, we've been able to achieve that goal and more since 2010. We'd like to thank our very dedicated fanbase for their endless support and constantly throwing money at us. At least we're better than DC. You mean the world to us and we hope to entertain you again. Thank you.
